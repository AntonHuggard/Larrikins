ABOUT

Larrikins has a long and proud history.

It was first published in the prestigious Salient (2018). It had the honour of appearing alongside Gus Mitchell's award-winning visual essay Trigger Fin. Then, out of no where, the page reserved for the Larrikins Christmas Special was vicously snatched by the season finale of Trigger Fin. Ever since, Trigger Fin has been the sworn enemy of Larrikins.

All the comics have been written and drawn by me. You're welcome.
